Homeward

We're an overflow pour
of people, a random crowd

of rain-glittered coats, yet
we streamline and board the bus step

by step—a single-file
promise each makes to the other,

leaning homeward like herons
easing wing by wing

together over shining tidelands,
over outbursts of evergreens.
