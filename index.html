About

This is Joseph Matthew Tate's website, or, as I'm known to family, friends, colleagues and coworkers: Jody Tate.

I live in Kirkland, WA and work as an Interface Designer and Developer for the Applications Engineering unit of Information Technology at the University of Washington. I'm also Partner and Chief Design Officer at A Cleaner Internet. We make browser extensions, mobile apps and other cool things.

I have a Ph.D. in English Literature from the University of Washington. You can read my dissertation, Shakespeare, Prose and Verse: Unreadable Forms (1.14mb PDF file). I also edited The Music and Art of Radiohead and published an essay that appears in Radiohead and Philosophy.
